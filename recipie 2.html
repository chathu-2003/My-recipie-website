Salayoo Maluwa (Anchovy Curry)

Ingredients:

  • 250g fresh salayoo fish (cleaned and washed)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger (grated)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp roasted curry powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp tamarind juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them pop.
  2. Add onion, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add chili powder, turmeric, curry powder, and black pepper. Stir well.
  4. Gently add the salayoo fish and mix carefully.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and tamarind juice. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  7. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot with rice.
