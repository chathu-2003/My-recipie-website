Waw Malu Ambulthiyal (Catfish Sour Curry)

Ingredients

  • 500g Waw Malu (Catfish – cleaned and cut into chunks)
  • 1 cup Goraka (soaked in warm water)
  • 1 tsp Triphala (optional)
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds or powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 pieces of rampe (pandan leaves)
  • 1/2 cup water

Preparation Method

  1. Grind the goraka with a bit of its soaking water to form a paste.
  2. In a cooking pan, mix the goraka paste, spice powders, salt, and 1/2 cup water.
  3. Add the Waw Malu pieces into the pan and gently coat them with the mixture.
  4. Place the rampe on top and cook on low heat without stirring too much.
  5. Simmer uncovered for about 30 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed and the fish is well cooked.
  6. Serve warm with rice for a traditional Sri Lankan meal.
