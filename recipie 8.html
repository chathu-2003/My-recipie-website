Thalapath Malu (Swordfish Curry)

Ingredients

  • 500g Thalapath (Swordfish) – cleaned and cut into cubes
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp roasted curry powder
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 2 pieces goraka (soaked)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Preparation Method

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Add onions, green chilies, curry leaves, and cinnamon; sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add chili powder, turmeric, curry powder, pepper, and fenugreek seeds. Stir well.
  3. Add goraka and salt. Then add swordfish pieces and gently mix to coat with the spices.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil. Then reduce heat and simmer on low for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Cook until fish is tender and curry thickens slightly. Stir gently to avoid breaking the fish.
  6. Serve hot with rice and other sides like dhal or sambol.
