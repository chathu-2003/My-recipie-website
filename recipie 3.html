Sri Lankan Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh fish (cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 piece pandan leaf (rampe)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, curry leaves, and pandan leaf until fragrant.
  2. Add turmeric, chili powder, and curry powder. Stir for a minute.
  3. Add fish pieces and mix gently to coat with spices.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add salt to taste. Let it cook for about 15–20 minutes or until fish is tender.
  6. Serve hot with rice or roti. Enjoy!
