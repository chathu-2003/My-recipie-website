Saman Maluwa (Salmon Curry)

Ingredients:

  • 500g fresh salmon (cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger (grated)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp tamarind juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  2. Add onion, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until golden.
  3. Add chili powder, turmeric, curry powder, and black pepper. Stir well.
  4. Add the salmon pieces and mix gently.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and tamarind juice. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  7. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot with rice.
