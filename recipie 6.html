Aguno Ambulthiyal (Silver Belly Fish Sour Curry)

Ingredients

  • 500g Aguno (Silver belly fish – cleaned and gutted)
  • 1/2 cup Goraka (soaked in warm water)
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (crushed or ground)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 pieces of rampe (pandan leaves)
  • 2 garlic cloves (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup water

Preparation Method

  1. Grind the goraka into a smooth paste using some of the soaking water.
  2. In a clay or non-stick pan, mix goraka paste, all spice powders, crushed mustard, salt, and water.
  3. Add the cleaned Aguno fish and mix gently to coat evenly.
  4. Add garlic slices and rampe over the top.
  5. Cook on low heat for about 25–30 minutes until the liquid reduces and the fish is firm.
  6. Do not stir while cooking. Let it cool and serve with warm rice.
