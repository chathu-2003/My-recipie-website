Bolla Ambulthiyal (Sour Fish Curry)

Ingredients

  • 500g Bolla fish (cleaned and cut)
  • 1 cup Goraka (soaked in water)
  • 1 tsp Triphala (optional spice blend)
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground fenugreek (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 pieces of rampe (pandan leaves)
  • 1/2 cup water

Preparation Method

  1. Combine all the spice powders, goraka, salt, and water in a clay pot or thick-bottomed pan.
  2. Add the pieces of Bolla fish and mix gently.
  3. Cook over low heat. Avoid adding oil; let it cook naturally.
  4. Simmer for 25–30 minutes until well-cooked and flavors are absorbed.
  5. Once done, serve hot with rice for the best traditional taste.
