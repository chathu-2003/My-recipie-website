Goldfish

Goldfish (Carassius auratus) are one of the most popular and beloved pet fish worldwide. Originally domesticated in China over a thousand years ago, they are known for their bright colors, adaptability, and long lifespan. With proper care, goldfish can live up to 20 years!

Physical Characteristics:

  • Size: 6–8 inches (15–20 cm), can grow up to 12–14 inches (30–35 cm).
  • Lifespan: Up to 20 years in optimal conditions.
  • Colors: Orange, red, black, yellow, and white.

Care Requirements:

  • Tank Size: Minimum 20 gallons (75 liters) per fish.
  • Water Temperature: 18–24°C (65–75°F).
  • Filtration: Required for clean water and healthy fish.
