Kiri Maluwo (Fish in Coconut Milk)

Ingredients

  • 500g white fish (such as Thalapath or Modha – cleaned and cut)
  • 1 ½ cups thick coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup thin coconut milk
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 inch piece of cinnamon
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Method

  1. In a pot, combine thin coconut milk, onion, green chilies, turmeric, fenugreek, curry leaves, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Bring it to a gentle simmer on medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the fish pieces and let it cook for another 10–15 minutes until the fish is cooked through.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the thick coconut milk. Do not let it boil after adding thick milk.
  5. Cook for another 2–3 minutes, gently spooning curry over fish to absorb the flavors.
  6. Serve warm with rice, pol sambol, or parippu (dal curry).
